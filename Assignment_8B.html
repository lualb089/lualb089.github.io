Earth to Hit Critical Warming Threshold by
Early 2030s, Climate Panel Says!

A new U.N. report says it is still possible to hold global warming to relatively safe levels, but
doing so will require global cooperation, billions of dollars and big changes.

Earth is likely to cross a critical threshold for global warming within the next decade, and nations
will need to make an immediate and drastic shift away from fossil fuels to prevent the planet from
overheating dangerously beyond that level, according to a major new report released on Monday.
